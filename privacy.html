Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Our Approach

Just Fed! is built to keep baby tracking simple and private. Core tracking is local-first, and we only use networked services where they are needed to provide specific features like family sharing, authentication, and subscriptions.

In short: we do not run ads, we do not sell your data, and we do not use Just Fed! to build a marketing profile about you.

What We Do Not Do

We do not show ads inside Just Fed!

We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers

We do not use your baby tracking history for advertising or cross-app profiling

We do not treat every feature as cloud-only. Core tracking is designed to remain local-first.

What Stays on Your Device

Everyday tracking data is centered on your iPhone. Just Fed! is designed so the core experience remains fast, direct, and usable without turning every interaction into a cloud request.

  • Your logged feeds, sleep, nappies, timers, and app state are stored locally for the core app experience.
  • If you never use family sharing, your day-to-day tracking can stay a single-device experience.
  • Deleting the app removes locally stored app data from your device.

When Data Is Sent to Our Services

Some features require network services to work correctly. When you use those features, the app sends the information needed to provide them.

Family sharing

Shared timelines depend on backend sync so both phones can stay aligned. If you create or join a family, relevant event history and updates are transmitted to our backend to support syncing between family members.

Authentication

Just Fed! uses Firebase anonymous authentication so the app can identify a device account for protected backend features like family sharing and subscription checks.

Subscriptions

Premium purchases and activation use RevenueCat together with our backend subscription status checks so premium features can unlock correctly.

Sync notifications

Device tokens and push infrastructure may be used to notify linked devices that new shared timeline data is available.

Third-Party Services Used to Run the App

Just Fed! does use third-party infrastructure where it is necessary to operate specific features. These services are used to run the product, not to monetize your behavior.

  • Firebase for anonymous authentication and related app backend access.
  • RevenueCat for subscription and premium entitlement handling.
  • Our own backend service for family sync, invite codes, subscription status checks, and push coordination.

Security and Access

We try to keep access to synced features limited to authenticated app users and to only store what is needed to provide those features.

No system can promise absolute security, but the app and backend are designed to protect family sync and subscription access rather than expose your data for broad commercial use.

Your Choices

  • You can use Just Fed! without enabling family sharing.
  • You can cancel premium through Apple subscription settings.
  • Deleting the app removes local data from your device. If you have used synced family features, backend data related to those features may also exist as part of the shared timeline service.

Children's Privacy

Just Fed! is built for parents and caregivers to track baby activity. We do not knowingly build child profiles for advertising or analytics purposes. The information entered into the app is controlled by the adult using it.

Changes to This Policy

The Bottom Line

Just Fed! is privacy-first, not privacy-fiction. Core tracking is local-first, premium and family features use the services needed to work, and we do not use your data as an advertising product.
