Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At JustFed, we believe your family's data should stay private. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information—or more accurately, how we don't.

In short: We don't collect, track, sell, or share your personal data. No user or personal information is ever stored. Your baby's activity logs belong to you and you alone.

What We Don't Collect

We don't collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers

We don't track your location or device information

We don't use analytics or tracking tools

We don't sell your data to advertisers or third parties

We don't show ads or use your data for marketing

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into JustFed—feeding logs, sleep times, nappy changes—is stored locally on your iPhone. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable sharing
  • No personal or user information is ever stored by us
  • Deleting the app removes all local data from your device

Sharing Between Devices

If you choose to enable sharing, JustFed allows you to sync your logs across multiple devices using a unique PIN. Here's how it works:

  • You must explicitly enable sharing—it's not automatic
  • When enabled, a unique PIN is generated for your devices to connect
  • Only your activity logs (feeds, sleep, nappies) are stored in our database when sharing is enabled
  • No personal or user data is stored—only the activity logs themselves
  • Data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure
  • You can disable sharing at any time to stop syncing

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure for sharing functionality. Supabase is a trusted, secure platform for data storage. You can learn more about their security practices at Supabase Security.

Data Security

Your data on your device is protected by iOS's built-in security features. When sharing is enabled, your activity logs are securely stored using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. No personal or identifying information is ever transmitted or stored.

Your Control

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can view all logged entries at any time
  • You can disable sharing at any time to stop syncing
  • Deleting the app removes all local data from your device

Children's Privacy

JustFed is designed for parents to track their baby's activities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data entered is controlled by the parent or guardian using the app.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is Supabase for optional data syncing when sharing is enabled. We do not integrate with any analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party tracking tools.

The Bottom Line

Your baby's data is precious and private. We built JustFed with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought. You can use our app with confidence knowing that your family's information stays with you, where it belongs.
