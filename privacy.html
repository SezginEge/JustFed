Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At JustFed, we believe your family's data should stay private. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information—or more accurately, how we don't.

In short: We don't collect, track, sell, or share your personal data. Your baby's information belongs to you and you alone.

What We Don't Collect

  • ✓ We don't collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers
  • ✓ We don't track your location or device information
  • ✓ We don't use analytics or tracking tools
  • ✓ We don't sell your data to advertisers or third parties
  • ✓ We don't show ads or use your data for marketing

How Your Data is Stored

All data you enter into JustFed—feeding logs, sleep times, nappy changes—is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's CoreData framework. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to pair with a partner
  • We have no access to your data
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • If you delete the app, all local data is deleted with it

Pairing and CloudKit Sync

If you choose to pair with your partner, JustFed uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data between devices. Here's how it works:

  • You must explicitly opt in to pairing—it's not automatic
  • Only paired events (feeds, sleep, nappies) are synced via CloudKit
  • CloudKit data is encrypted and stored in Apple's secure cloud infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your CloudKit data
  • You can unpair at any time to stop syncing

Apple's CloudKit service is subject to Apple's own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Apple's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle cloud data.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and passcode protection. When using CloudKit for pairing, data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can delete any entry at any time
  • You can export your data for your own records
  • You can delete the app and all associated data
  • You can unpair from your partner to stop syncing

Children's Privacy

JustFed is designed for parents to track their baby's activities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data entered is controlled by the parent or guardian using the app.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is Apple's CloudKit for optional data syncing between paired devices. We do not integrate with any analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how JustFed handles your data, please contact us:

Email: support@justfed.app

The Bottom Line

Your baby's data is precious and private. We built JustFed with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought. You can use our app with confidence knowing that your family's information stays with you, where it belongs.
